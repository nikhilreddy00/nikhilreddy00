### Hey there! 👋

I'm **Nikhil** — an ML Engineer who builds AI systems that work in production, not just in notebooks.

Over the past 3+ years, I've gone from data analysis → NLP modeling → full-stack ML engineering across **healthcare** and **e-commerce**. Currently at **George Washington University's Milken Institute School of Public Health**, where I build RAG pipelines over 15K+ clinical records, fine-tune language models (BERT, Llama via LoRA), and deploy containerized inference endpoints on AWS SageMaker.

I'm completing my **MS in Data Analytics** (May 2026) and actively building in the LLM/agentic AI space.
